Objectives

Since its early beginning, the purpose of the Union has been to advance the development of the basic and clinical neurosciences by:


  • The dissemination of information and scientific knowledge in neurosciences.
  • Stimulating, encouraging and developing programs of clinical and basic neurological research throughout the Arab Countries.
  • Providing means of a closer professional and personal appreciation of Arab neurologists and neurological societies.
  • The contribution to active international neurological congresses specially: European Academy of Neurology (EAN), American Academy of Neurology (AAN), and World Federation of Neurology (WFN).
